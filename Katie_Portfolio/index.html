A Snippet About Me.

Hi there! My name is Katelyn Dawn Sickles, but you can call me Katelyn, Katie, Dawn, and your new hairstylist. Based in Vancouver, WA, I graduated from Aveda in 2021 and have been making hair dreams come true ever since. As a people-first hair stylist, I am happy to do whatever it takes to get you to your goals, but I specialize in fantasy colors, unique shave designs, and intricate braids. I am your hairy godmother.
